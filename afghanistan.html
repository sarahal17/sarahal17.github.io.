AFGHANISTAN

With the return of the Taliban after years of war many people are being displaced from their homes, having not enough funds to support their family, as well as the new installation of the government to not allow women to go to school past the 6th grade. Under the new government of the Islamic Emirate of Afghanistan or the Taliban , the people are experiencing extreme poverty, increase in COVID cases, no forms of health care as basic care is being shut down and women and girls are being more susceptible to abuse, restrictions based on gender and as well as child marriages.
